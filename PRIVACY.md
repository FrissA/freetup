# Privacy Policy

**Freetup** is a browser extension that removes paywalls on meetup.com.

## Data Collection

Freetup does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or user information to external servers.

## Storage Permission

The extension uses the browser's local `storage` API solely to save your preferences on your device. This data never leaves your browser.

## Third Parties

No data is shared with or sent to any third parties.
